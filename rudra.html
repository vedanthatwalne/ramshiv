    Rudrabhishek/Laghurudra Abhishek

    Rudrabhisheka is a ritual in which a Panchamrut is offered to Lord Shiva along with mantras to grant all of the wishes of the individual who performs it. This Rudrabhisheka bestows wealth, the satisfaction of all desires, the removal of negativity, the cutting off of bad karma, and overall Happiness. The devotees enchant mantras/Shlokas for communicating with God. By enchanting these mantras, the surroundings/environment is filled with positive vibes and creating positive energy in the minds of devotees. In the Rudrabhisheka Puja, 108 names of Lord Shiva are chanted. The Shiva Linga is decorated with Rudraksha.
