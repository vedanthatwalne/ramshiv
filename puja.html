    Narayanbali Nagbali

    This Vidhi is mainly done for the removing of Pitra Dosh, Childbirth Dosh, Wealth Accumulation Dosh, Disturbance Dosh, Home Dosh etc. This is a 3 days Vidhi.

    Tripindi Shraddha

    This Pooja is performed for those ancestors who have not performed Shradh for three consecutive years. This Shraddha is mainly done for Pitru Shanti. This is a method of two to three hours a day.

    Kalsarp Shanti

    This Pooja is mainly suitable for youth. This Pooja is done to get rid of problems in Education, Wealth and Job or Business, Marriage, etc. This is a Pooja of two to three hours a day.

    Grah Shanti etc.

    According to the horoscope, this Pooja is done in Trimbakeshwar to get rid of all the troubles that come. It is done for the prevention of various planetary defects (Grah Dosh), planetary conjunction defects (Grah Yuti Dosh), Nakshatra/Yoga/Karan defects. This is a Pooja of two to three hours a day.

    Rudrabhishek/Laghurudra Abhishek

    This Pooja is performed in the main temple of Lord Jyotirlinga Trimbakeshwar . This Pooja is mainly done to get the best of health, to fulfill the wishes, to get the special blessings of Trimbakeshwar Ji. This Pooja is completed in 2 to 3 hours with 1 to 11 brahmins.

    Mahamrityunjaya Mantra Jaap

    Mahamrityunjaya Mantra is chanted to please Lord Shiva. With the effect of this mantra, all kinds of diseases are cured. Negativity, fear and obstacles coming in the work are also removed by chanting the Mahamrityunjay mantra. If this mantra is chanted with full rituals, then even the biggest crisis is averted. If someone is suffering from a disease, there is a possibility of distribution of land and property, there is loss of wealth or if desired wishes are to be fulfilled, then this chanting is done.
