    Our Puja

    Narayanbali Nagbali
    This Vidhi is mainly done for the removing of Pitra Dosh, Childbirth Dosh, Wealth Accumulation Dosh, Disturbance Dosh, Home Dosh etc. This is a 3 days Vidhi.
    Tripindi Shraddha
    This Pooja is performed for those ancestors who have not performed Shradh for three consecutive years. This Shraddha is mainly done for Pitru Shanti. This is a method of two to three hours a day.
    Kalsarp Shanti
    This Pooja is mainly suitable for youth. This Pooja is done to get rid of problems in Education, Wealth and Job or Business, Marriage, etc. This is a Pooja of two to three hours a day.
