    Grah Shanti etc.

    It is a ritual which is performed to remove all the obstacles and bring happiness and prosperity into the lives of the person. As it is believed that the stars and the planets have a great influence on our lives, this pooja is performed to ensure that the Navagrah or 9 planets are aligned for both the bride and the groom in order to live a happy life together. Like the name suggests, Grah means the house and Shanti means peace, thus it means the peace of the house.

    Thing You Should Know?

    There are so many benefits of Narayan Nagbali Puja; Some are described below:
    •This Puja removes the negative effects of malefic planets.
    •Removes all hurdles and problems associated with misalignment of the planets.
    •It greatly enhances the positive effects of well aligned planets.
    •This Puja helps to give one a healthy, wealthy and prosperous life.

    Our life is greatly influenced by the placement of planets or Grahas in the natal chart. There are nine Grahas/Planets that influence almost every aspect of a person’s life. The various Grahs includes Surya, Chandra, Mangal, Budh, Brihaspati, Shukra, Rahu, Ketu, and Shani. The influence exerted by these nine planets is referred to as "Dasha."
    The good or bad times in a person’s life are attributed to the placement of planets. Well aligned planets are the reason for fortunate events in one’s life and misaligned planets are responsible for unfortunate events.

    Each planet has its unique role in our lives and sometimes when the planets are in a retrograde or malefic alignment they affect us in an inauspicious manner which may in turn effect our career, finances, relationships, love/marital life and health. This Puja is performed to remove the negative effects/doshas of the malefic planets and appease them so that one’s life can be free of hurdles and hardships.

Ensures to prevent any unfortunate events from taking place to the person or the family who conducts the Puja.
