    Temple - About Shri Kshetra Trimbakeshwar

    सह्याद्रिशीर्षे विमले वसन्तं गोदावरितीरपवित्रदेशे |

    यद्दर्शनात्पातकमाशु नाशं प्रयाति तं त्र्यम्बकमीशमीडे ||

    Trimbakeshwar Jyotirlinga is an ancient pilgrimage site in India. The reason why it is special out of all the 12 Jyotirlingas is that there are three sexes of the creator Brahma, the protector Vishnu and the protector Shiva in one place. This village is at a distance of 28 km from Nashik city. The source of the holy river Ganga Godavari is on the Brahmagiri mountain of Trimbakeshwar. There is a Kushavart shrine near the temple premises. Shri Dnyaneshwar Mauli's elder brother Shri Nivruttinath Maharaj's Samadhi is also in Trimbakeshwar. Also the confluence of Godavari and Ahilya rivers is to the east of the temple. Where Satismashan is. Simhastha Kumbh Mela, which is very sacred in Hinduism and has a unique general significance, is also held here.
